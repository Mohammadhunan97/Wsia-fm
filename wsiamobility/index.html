Program Underwriting

WSIA is a non-commercial, student- run broadcaster that reaches nearly 500,000 people on a daily basis. Unlike so many of the other New York stations, we are focused on Staten Island - an area often forgotten by both media outlets and numerous companies. We offer sponsored spots to members of the local community.

Common Faqs

Where is WSIA located?

2800 Victory Boulevard, Building 1C Room 106, Staten Island, NY 10314

Do I have to pay to join WSIA?

As a alumni volunteer there is a small yearly fee of $25. As a CSI student, membership to the radio station is already paid for through a small percentage of your student activity fees

What does the radio station require of me?

All members must spend 2 hours a week doing some kind of station work and his can be done in the department of your choosing (Can't commit to 2 hours a week? You can still be a member, though some privileges may be temporarily suspended as per the decision of the General Manager).

Do station members get paid?

Unfortunately, regular members are not paid. Though exceptional members may be rewarded through our scholarship fund. In addition we have opportunities through our business department to earn commision.
